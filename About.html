ABOUT US

ROACHWORLD NETWORKING & SERVICES LTD. is a multi-dimensional and multi-capacity IC- Telecom services provider and a full data center ISP (Internet Service Provider).

ROACHWORLD NETWORKING & SERVICES LTD. was established to offer a tailored approach and dedicated broad telecom supports services to meet businesses with diverse communications needs to boost their business productivity and continuity.

Zappen Technology Ltd, our subsidiary, offer topnotch and reliable internet services to all domestic and foreign internet users, both individuals or companies and aim to provide the latest technologies in the fields of Internet, Communications, Telecommunications, fibre Optical solutions; this also include End to End connectivity with last mile patching through direct fiber wireless and Microwave links connectivity.

Additionally ROACHWORLD NETWORKING & SERVICES LTD. offers supports related services of Preventive and corrective maintenance services for the telecom towers and cabins. Our History We proud to say that ROACHWORLD NETWORKING & SERVICES LTD is a 100% Nigerian company founded in 2015 by a young man who have huge passion for IC-telecoms as very young man who can be refered to as a gifted hand and brain of this generation. He started off as NOC centre hand and over the years he developed himself into multi competent hand in the IC-Telecoms space, with strength in the passive subspace of the telecom sector.
